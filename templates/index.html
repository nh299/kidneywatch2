Dialysis Prevention

Our Mission

We aim at preventing Dialysis for kindey disease patients at stages 3 and 4.
By using our food tracking software, we hope we can preserve a better standard of living for Kidney Disease patients

A Silent Killer

Aproximately 33% of the population are at high risk for kidney disease. That is 1 in 3 people! For these individuals, 90% of kidney function can be lost before symptoms appear.

Importance of Diet Changes

About 50% of dialysis patients might have been able to prevent ESRD through diet and lifestyle changes. With a proper diet people can allow their kidney to stay healthier for longer.
